Confectionery Cannon

The Confectionery Cannon. We made it with computers, and math.

We used many of Olin's fabrication resources, including the mills, lathes, and laser cutters.

After sketching out a few initial designs, we designed the entire system in Solidworks. Most of the parts are acrylic, which we chose primarily for its cost and ease of laser-cutting. Acrylic isn't very strong and is quite bittle, but it's less than a third of the cost of acetyl (Delrin). The three aluminium parts were made from shop scrap.
